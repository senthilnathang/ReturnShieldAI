03/13 The attack surface

10 fraud patterns that legacy tools can't detect

📦

Empty box scam

Customer claims item missing, returns empty packaging with original weight faked.

⚖️

Weight fraud

Returned package weighs significantly less than shipped — partial contents returned.

👗

Wardrobing

Buy, wear once, return. Classic apparel fraud invisible to checkout tools.

🔄

Price arbitrage

Buy on discount, return with higher-priced receipt for profit.

🗣️

Fake return narratives

Scripted chat transcripts and emails pushing for immediate refunds.

🔗

Organized fraud rings

Networks of accounts sharing addresses, devices, payment methods across returns.

📸

Photo manipulation

Fake damage photos or reused photos across multiple return requests.

📋

Label reuse / shipping fraud

Same shipping label used across multiple returns from different accounts.

🧾

Receipt / invoice fraud

Returning items purchased elsewhere or at different prices.
