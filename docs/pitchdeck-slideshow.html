The problem

Legacy fraud stacks miss the returns layer.

The gap

Checkout fraud systems are optimized for cards and chargebacks. Returns include shipping, text, images, and account behavior those systems do not fully inspect.

What attackers exploit

Empty-box claims, weight fraud, wardrobing, price arbitrage, fake narratives, and organized account rings.

Accuracy

Accuracy comes from layered validation, not black-box claims.

What accuracy means

Precision on obvious fraud, recall on subtle fraud, consistency across repeated cases, and reviewability for analysts and auditors.

Built-in safeguards

Ensemble scoring, explainability, feedback capture, and thresholding keep the system measurable and easy to tune.

What the repo supports today

  • Score outputs on a 0-100 scale
  • Score breakdown by signal type
  • Precision, recall, and F1 tracked in training
